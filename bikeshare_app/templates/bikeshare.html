Binghamton University Bike Share

Binghamton University Bike Share (BUBS) is BU’s own bike share program. Spread the word to all of your friends!

Hours

Hours are 12:00-8:00 PM.

How it Works?

  1. Sign out a free bike at the Old Union Info Desk. You will be issued a key with a number on it.
  2. Walk out of the Union to the front of the Engineering Building. You will see 2 bike racks under the overhang with our bikes on them.
  3. Find the bike with the same number found on your key, and unlock it.
  4. Enjoy your free bike for a maximum of 4 hours. Available times are from 12 pm – 8 pm
  5. Return the bike to our rack when you are done. Make sure it is properly locked up.
  6. Return the key to the Old Union Info Desk and sign the bike back in.
